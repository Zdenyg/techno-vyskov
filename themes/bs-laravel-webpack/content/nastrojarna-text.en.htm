The main activity of the tool shop is the production of molds for plastic injection.

We also offer cooperation in individual professions according to the machinery and the production of spare shaped parts for molds.

We are able to repair damaged parts of molds by laser welding and subsequent machining of the weld.

Construction of molds in 3D and 2D is performed on CAD workplaces UGS - input formats STEP, IGES, etc.
